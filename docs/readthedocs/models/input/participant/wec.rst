.. _wec_cp_characteristic:

Betz Characteristic
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
A collection of wind velocity to Betz factor pairs to be applied in
`Betz's law <https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Betz's_law>`_ to determine the wind energy coming onto the rotor area.
